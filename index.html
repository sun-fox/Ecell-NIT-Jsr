ABOUT US

E-cell, NIT Jamshedpur is a non profit student run body with the mission to provide various opportunities to entrepreneurship enthusiasts. E-Cell NIT, Jamshedpur under Dean R &C by Paresh Vishnu, Rajat Singh , Dushyant Chaudhary and Akshat Mittal from 2k16. The cell is currently run and assisted by third and second year undergraduates of the institute.

Our Vision

To create a sustainable and easily accessible environment where the idea from the quality brains could outreach to building professionals, mentors, venture capitalists and angel investors fostering the growth of startups in India.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide an ambient atmosphere to the aspiring students about the start-up culture and grow their business by creating a pathway of various opportunities like interactive sessions with business professionals, business competitions to increase the awareness. This will help foster entrepreneurial thinking, promote business, knowledge, and instill confidence in students.

Our Achievements

Within a year of it's foundation the students of Nit Jamshedpur had an opportunity to represent their institute at state level and there bagged 1st & 3rd prize in technology sector during start-up India Jharkhand Event.

What We Do ?

We helps students to convert their dreams into reality, develop their entrepreneurship skills & create enthusiasm towards the start-up ecosystem. E-Cell NIT Jamshedpur is a team of around fifty hardworking and devoted individuals, who are always working towards our mission and goals. We function as six diversified groups but are always united at the base.

Corp. Relations & Networking

  • Our corporate relation team manages the actions that shapes perceptions of E-Cell NIT, Jsr & develop relationships with students, incubators, angel investors, and other important stakeholders so that both can be mutually benefited.
  • Corporate Relations & Networking team spans a broad array of tactics and strategies which involves: 1.Media relations 2.Investor relations 3.Public affairs 4.Connections with Entrepreneurs
  • Corporate Relations & Marketing plans are more comprehensive & designed to help E-Cell NIT, jsr to achieve its core goals and bring sponsors for our team.
  • Corporate Relation & Networking department is the point of contact for the media. Their work includes writing and distributing news releases & responding to various inquires.

  • Follow E-cell NIT, Jsr at various platforms to connect with us: Facebook- entrepreneurship.cell.nit.jsr LinkedIn-LinkedIn link Instagram-ecell.nitjsr Twitter-Twitter link E-mail-ecell@nitjsr.ac.in
  • Social Media & Marketing is a resource through which our organization is connected with students, entrepreneurs, professionals and several other enthusiasts.
  • Promotion of events, information regarding various upcoming events such as Workshops, Bootcamps, Biz-words, BizQuest, Success story, E-Talk, etc.
  • They oversee all planning for news conferences, including selecting the site for an event, preparing packets of information to display at E-cell websites, facebook & Instagram account.

Web Development

  • E-Cell Web Team is a group of passionate programmers and web developers who are responsible for the development and maintenance of the E-Cell NIT Jamshedpur's Website.
  • We do tasks that involve UI/UX designing, front-end development, backend development and a bit of SEO optimization. E-Cell's web development team consists of both full-stack developers and dedicated specialists working with a wide spectrum of technologies including HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, Figma, PHP, NodeJS, MongoDB, Express and others. The entire website is being designed and developed by us.

App development

  • E-Cell App Development Team is a group of individuals who have an expertise in App development on the Android Studio IDE.

Operations
